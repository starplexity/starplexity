Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

Information We Collect

We collect non-personal analytics data necessary for game functionality and performance improvement, such as crash reports, device model, and operating system. We do not store or process personally identifiable information unless provided voluntarily via our Contact Form. This data helps us ensure game stability and deliver a better user experience without infringing on personal privacy.

Use of Third-Party Services

Our games integrate services like Google Play and various ad network partners to support free-to-play content. These partners operate under their respective privacy policies. We take proactive steps to ensure data shared with them is anonymized and aggregated where possible, minimizing any potential personal exposure.

Data Security and Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the non-personal data we collect. We retain this data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Your Rights

Users have the right to request access to and deletion of any personal data we may hold (if any, as we primarily collect non-personal data). Please contact our privacy officer at privacy@gamestudio.com for such requests or if you have any questions regarding this policy.
